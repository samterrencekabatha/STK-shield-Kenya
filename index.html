The Problem Facing Kenya

M-Pesa transformed Africa's financial ecosystem. But the current Buy Goods and STK push flow allows any merchant or customer to enter any phone number without identity verification. This creates fraud opportunities, accidental payment requests, and daily notification fatigue for millions of users.

⚠️

Fraud Attempts

Fraudsters intentionally input random numbers hoping users accidentally approve payments while distracted or tired.

📱

Wrong Number Entries

Genuine customers mistype phone numbers, causing innocent users to receive unexpected STK push prompts.

🔔

Notification Fatigue

Constant unrelated prompts create mental clutter, anxiety, distraction, and reduce trust in payment systems.

Proposed Solution Features

✅

Trusted Merchants

Merchants you regularly transact with are automatically approved while new merchants require additional verification.

🛡️

Smart Verification

First-time merchants trigger enhanced security prompts and location-based fraud analysis.

🚫

Merchant Blocking

Users permanently block problematic tills, paybills, or merchants from future STK push attempts.

🧠

AI Fraud Detection

Detect suspicious patterns such as repeated failed pushes, unusual locations, or high-risk merchant activity.
