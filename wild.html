Kenya's Vibrant Marine Life

Beyond the savannah lies a hidden underwater paradise. Kenya’s marine ecosystems are home to coral reefs, dolphins, sea turtles, manta rays and vibrant tropical fish species that contribute to the country's ecological richness.

This immersive visual gallery uses layered card interactions to create excitement and showcase marine conservation storytelling. Every interaction supports awareness campaigns against ocean pollution and illegal fishing.

Donate Against Poaching

Illegal poaching threatens Kenya’s wildlife heritage every year. Your support helps fund ranger patrols, wildlife rescue missions, community education programs and advanced animal tracking systems protecting endangered species.
